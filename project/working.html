HemoBank is a simple digital platform that connects blood donors, blood banks, and people in need. Our mission is to make blood donation and access faster, easier, and more transparent so that no life is lost for lack of blood.

‚ù§ Steps to Donate Blood:

  1. Visit HemoBank and click on Register as Donor.
  2. Fill in your personal information and blood group details.
  3. Choose a nearby blood bank for donation from the options.
  4. Schedule your donation date and reach the selected center on time.
  5. After donation, you will receive confirmation and a thank-you message.

‚ù§ Steps to Receive Blood:

  1. Go to HemoBank and click on Request Blood.
  2. Enter the required details: patient name, blood group, and your location/contact info.
  3. The system will notify nearby donors and registered blood banks.
  4. When a match is found, the donor or blood bank will contact you directly.
  5. Visit the hospital or blood bank to collect the blood as instructed.
